Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2026

Welcome to GRIEVE - Dating for Downers ("we," "our," or "the App"). Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information that you provide directly to us, including:

  • Profile information (username, age, avatar selection, bio, emotional journey, looking for)
  • Gender identity and preferences
  • Photos you take or upload using your device camera (with your permission)
  • Messages and chat invites you send and receive
  • Graffiti wall posts, comments, and reactions
  • Subscription and payment information (processed by third-party payment providers)
  • Grieve Lord nominations and votes (for Silent Order members)
  • Device information and usage data

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve our services
  • Match you with other users based on your preferences
  • Enable communication between matched users
  • Process subscription payments
  • Detect and prevent ghosting behavior
  • Send you notifications (with your permission)
  • Enforce our Terms of Service and protect user safety
  • Manage the Grieve Lord nomination and voting system

3. Information Sharing

We may share your information with:

  • Other users as part of your profile and interactions
  • Service providers who assist in app operations
  • Law enforcement when required by law
  • In connection with a business transfer or acquisition
IMPORTANT: Information you share in your profile, messages, and graffiti posts can be seen by other users. Be mindful of what you share publicly.

4. Data Storage and Security

We store your data on secure servers and implement reasonable security measures. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We use local device storage for certain data, and you are responsible for securing your device.

5. Your Privacy Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal information
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Delete your account and data
  • Hide your profile from other users
  • Control notification settings

6. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. When you delete your account, we will delete or anonymize your personal information within 30 days, except where retention is required by law.

7. Children's Privacy

Our service is only available to users 18 years and older. We do not knowingly collect information from anyone under 18. If we discover that a user is under 18, we will delete their account immediately.

8. Camera Permissions

We request camera permissions to allow you to take profile photos and share images within the app. We only access your camera when you explicitly choose to use the camera feature. Photos are stored locally on your device and on our servers only if you choose to upload them to your profile or share them with other users.

9. Third-Party Services

We use third-party services for payments and analytics. These services have their own privacy policies and terms. We are not responsible for their privacy practices.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes through the app. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

11. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through the app settings or at the email provided in the app store listing.
